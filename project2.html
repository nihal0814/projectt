Why We Do Aeronautical Engineering

We believe that every young enthusiast has the capability of making something new and innovative. Team Griffin is the home where we get to make and explore a world never seen before, and what it takes for your model to reach the skies.

How We Do Aeronautical Engineering

Our experienced team has been internally assigned to various domains that work cohesively to bring our models to reality. All analysis is done in-house to prove and validate designs, which are then handed over to the manufacturing and avionics experts to produce the aircraft.
